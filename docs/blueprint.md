# **App Name**: SafeHaven

## Core Features:

- Incident Reporter: A customizable form for users to safely and anonymously report GBV incidents.
- Emergency Guidance: Provide real-time, life-saving instructions and connect users with emergency hotlines.
- Legal Rights Info: Educate survivors about their legal rights and the procedures involved in reporting GBV.
- Referral Tool: A system to link survivors to relevant help centers and support resources in their vicinity.
- Anonymous Community Board: A local anonymous online community discussion board
- Safe location finder: Tool to allow the LLM to use various location based tools to determine and present near by safe locations

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Calming light blue (#A0D2EB) to evoke trust and security.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E9F5FF) for a serene and supportive environment.
- Accent color: Soft lavender (#B39DDB) to highlight important information and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, for clear, accessible information.
- Use clean, minimalist icons to represent different types of resources and actions.
- Ensure a clear and intuitive layout that guides users through each step, emphasizing ease of use and quick access to essential features.
- Implement subtle, reassuring animations for feedback and loading states to enhance user experience without overwhelming them.
